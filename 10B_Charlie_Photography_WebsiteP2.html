Filipino Photographers

Explore the works of some of the most renowned Filipino photographers who have captured moments that transcend borders, cultures, and time.

Xyza Cruz Bacani

Xyza Cruz Bacani is a Filipina photographer known for her work documenting migrant workers and human rights issues. She was named one of Time Magazine's '100 Most Influential People' in 2018.

BJ Pascual

BJ Pascual is a renowned Filipino photographer known for his portraits and celebrity photography. He has worked with top fashion magazines and global brands, making him one of the Philippines' leading photographers.

Eloisa Lopez

Eloisa Lopez is an award-winning photojournalist whose work focuses on conflict, human rights, and social justice. Her photography has been published internationally, and she has covered major news events around the world.

Mark Nicdao

Mark Nicdao is a Filipino photographer renowned for his high-fashion work and celebrity portraits. He is recognized for his artistic vision and his contributions to both local and international fashion photography.

Worldwide Photographers

Explore the works of some of the most renowned global photographers.

Ansel Adams

Ansel Adams was an American photographer and environmentalist known for his black-and-white images of the American West. His work is iconic for its sharp details and grandeur.

Henri Cartier-Bresson

Henri Cartier-Bresson was a French photographer considered one of the greatest masters of the art. His candid photography captured the 'decisive moment' of life in action, shaping modern photojournalism.

Steve McCurry

Steve McCurry is an American photographer best known for his photograph 'Afghan Girl', which appeared on the cover of National Geographic. His photojournalism has brought global attention to the plight of refugees.

Richard Avedon

Richard Avedon was an American fashion and portrait photographer. His work revolutionized fashion photography with his innovative use of white backgrounds and candid portraits of famous personalities.
